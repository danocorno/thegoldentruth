Support Our Film

Your donation of time, money, and gently used equipment will be used to improve cancer care for children around the world. How? Awareness is the first step to solving a problem, so we must let the world know what's going on in the world of pediatric oncology in order to spark change. As more people become aware, more people will join in our fight for better treatments and eventually a cure. Every donation to The Golden Truth is tax-deductible and will be used to share the stories of kids fighting cancer!

other great organizations:

Basecamp Children's Cancer Foundation

Basecamp Children's Cancer Foundation serves children and families affected by childhood cancer in Central Florida. They provide year round support to children in 3 local hospitals. Some of their programs include food pantries, hospital meals, milestone parties, and their namesake BASECAMP overnight program!  

This Star Won't Go Out

Esther Grace Earl was a spunky, creative girl who passed away from cancer in 2010. Her family started TSWGO to serve families suffering from life-threatening childhood cancers. Since its founding in 2011, TSWGO has helped more than 200 families, with gifts totaling $300,000 to help families suffering from financial hardship related to childhood cancer.

St. Baldrick's Foundation

The St. Baldrick’s Foundation is a volunteer-powered charity committed to funding the most promising research to find cures for childhood cancers and give survivors long, healthy lives. Known for thier unique head shaving events, St. Baldricks is funding millions of dollars worth of childhood cancer research.  

Talia's Legacy Foundation

Talia Joy Castellano made her mark on the world, even after she gained her wings, she continues to influence millions through her social media platforms. Her mom Desiree and sister Mattia promise to carry out Talia’s wish to find a cure for childhood cancer. Talia’s Legacy will help to raise fund’s for clinical trials, research and drugs that specifically target childhood cancer.

That Lucky Bracelet

That Lucky Bracelet is a nonprofit organization that sends care packages to children in the hospital. High school sophomore Sophie Harris founded the program while in the hospital herself. Now, the teen sends "smile packages" to sick children around the world.

Give Hugs

Give Hugs is a organization that makes and delivers / ships blankets and care packages to kids and teens with cancer or other life threatening diseases. It is run entirely by a 17 year old teen who is chronically ill herself.

More organizations coming soon!
