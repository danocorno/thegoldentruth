Contact Us

Thank you for your interest in working with us, helping us, or getting to know us! We answer all emails within one business week, and prioritize our calls based on the most time sensitvie issues. Please include a subject line in your emails to help us sort them and get you in touch with the right people!

Questions?

I would love to get in touch with you! The best way to reach me is via email: nancy@thegoldentruth.org. Please expect a response between 2-3 business days. I look forward to hearing from you!

Interviews

Sharing your story is an important part of raising awareness. To apply for an interview, please fill out an interview application:

Patients & Families:

Thousands of children are diagnosed with childhood cancer every year. We believe that it is important to show their journey from every viewpoint. If you are a patient, family member, or friend- your story is important in the fight to raise awareness. Apply now!


Medical Professionals:

Whether it's customizing the perfect treatment plan, administering meds, or offering a hug- any pediatric oncology family will tell you how important medical professionals are to their journey. If you are a researcher, oncologist, nurse, child life specialist, medical assistant, tech, or anyone else who helps a child heal- apply now to share your story.


Charities:

We are so thakful for the wonderful organizations that make childhood cancer a little bit easier for kids and families. If you are involved in a charity that serves children with cancer, we would love to hear about your work! Please be aware that The Golden Truth is sponsored by Basecamp Children's Cancer Foundation, which serves children and families in Central Florida with year round support. Apply now to inspire change in the pediatric oncology community.

Once you sign up for an interview of any kind, click here for your next steps!


Other Inquires

To get in touch with our producers, please email Nancy Pickett.

For Businesses:

The Golden Truth is sponsored by BASE Camp Children's Cancer Foundation, a 501c3 charity. We accept grants and sponsorships from a number of businesses and corporations, and will include your advertisements as a part of our campaign depending on your donation. Please email Nancy with the subject "business inquiry" to get started.   


For Press:

The Golden Truth's producers will be traveling the country all year to film interviews and visit children's hospitals. For inquiries about interviews, news show appearances, and articles - email Nancy for scheduling. Click here for a press release


For Hospitals:

The Golden Truth will be visiting several major children's hospitals, and we are still accepting filming requests. If you are a media representative interested in hosting a filming session, please note that we only travel for hospitals that have confirmed 3+ interviews (families, medical professionals, etc.) and B-Roll opportunities. Some exceptions are made. At this time, we are only accepting inquiries from hospitals in the following states: Florida. Please email Nancy for a complete filming schedule.
