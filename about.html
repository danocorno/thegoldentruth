About The Golden Truth

The Golden Truth is a documentary film thatâ€™s uncovering the truth about pediatric oncology. We believe that showing the reality of what kids are facing will stimulate positive change in the childhood cancer community by raising awareness and bringing in funding for research. Our film will strip away the sugarcoat so often placed on pediatric cancer, without displaying childhood cancer as hopeless. We want to show the bad things that are happening, and give an approach to better solutions. The media displays childhood cancer as depressing images of dying children or happy bald kids at Disney World. Both of these have some truth to them, but alone, they are not the actual truth. There is hope in survivors, desperation and prayers for those who are in the fight, and grief for those who are gone- and all these things together make up pediatric cancer. By bringing awareness to every aspect of a family's journey, in addition to bringing in the perspectives of medical professionals, charities, and advocates, we strive to change the images that media has set in our minds about kids' cancer.

meet the producer

Nancy Pickett

Nancy Pickett is a high school senior with a passion for filmmaking. Nancy started The Golden Truth in 2013 after volunteering with BASECAMP Children's Cancer Foundation. Now, she has filmed hundreds of hours of interviews with childhood cancer patients, families, and medical professionals. She also delivers meals to children's hospitals several days a week with BASECAMP and is a regular chaperone at their overnight events.

FAQs:

When will the documentary be finished?
We are gathering stories from around the world about pediatric cancer awareness. Depending on the amount of funds we can raise, we would like to travel to different children's hospitals to continue collecting interviews and footage. Currently, our goal is to finish the film by Spring 2017 This is subject to change as we make travel plans.

How can I donate to help you in the production process?
Go to the "How you can help" tab, and click "Donate". You will be directed to our PayPal page. Cash and checks can be mailed to BASE Camp Children's Cancer Foundation **PLEASE NOTE** All checks must be made out to BASE Camp Children's Cancer Foundation, with The Golden Truth on the memo line. This is VERY important to make sure you recieve tax return.

What do you use donation money for?
We will use money donated to the film to help with various production costs, including but not limited to: equipment, travel, and distribution fees. For a more detailed description, see the help page.

Will you be coming to my child's hospital?
As we make our travel plans, we will release them here and on social media. To request that we come to your hospital, contact us.

How can I share my story?
Click on the "Contact" button, and send us an email! Please allow at least three business days for a response. All interviewees will need to sign our model release form, which allows us to use your voice/image in our film. If you are under 18, this form must be signed by a parent or legal gaurdian.

How should I prepare for my interview?
We will email you detailed instructions for your interview, and there are instructions listed on our Contact page.
