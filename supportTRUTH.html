the #supportTRUTH campaign

This year, we plan on making some big waves in the sea of childhood cancer awareness. We want to make the whole world aware! To make this happen, we have started the #supportTRUTH campaign. Each month, we will give you a way to help kids with cancer by raising awareness for the truth - what life is really like for a child with cancer. Post pictures of your challenges using the hashtag #supportTRUTH to tell everyone about your fight against pediatric cancer! Check back every month to get your challenge and to see our favorite posts.

January

Posted by Nancy Pickett, December 31, 2015

January is a time for resolutions. A resolution can be defined as a firm decision to do something - like eating right, working out, or learning something new. But it can also be defined as the action of solving a problem! This year let's solve the problem of childhood cancer awareness. Post your New Year's Resolution to #supportTRUTH now, for a chance to be featured on our page! Stay connected, invite your frineds, and let's make the world aware!

Check out our favorite posts from January!


February

Posted by Nancy Pickett, January 31, 2016

The ‪#‎supportTRUTH‬ campaign continues into February with our Heart of Gold challenge! This February, we challenge you to:

1. Wear black on Valentine's Day in honor of @taliajoy18

2. Raise awareness with gold roses, chocolates, and valentines!

3. Match your valentines spending with a donation to a childhood cancer charity - or sell candy grams at your school/work! See our website for some great charities to choose from.

4. Make Valentines for That Lucky Bracelet's Valentine's Day Card Drive! Visit That Lucky Bracelet for more info

You can do all of these things, or pick just one! Be sure to post how you #supportTRUTH with the hashtag for a chance to be featured on our page.
